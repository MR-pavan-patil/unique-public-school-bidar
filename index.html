Our School

A Legacy of Excellence in Bidar

Unique Public School has been a cornerstone of quality education in Bidar for over two and a half decades. We offer a nurturing environment where every child's potential is discovered, developed, and celebrated.

Our curriculum blends the CBSE framework with co-curricular activities, sports, arts, and value education — empowering students to thrive in an ever-changing world.
