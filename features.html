What We Offer

Beyond the Classroom

At Unique Public School, we believe education extends far beyond textbooks. Our diverse programs nurture talent, build confidence, and shape well-rounded individuals.

💻

Coding Classes

Master programming languages like Python, JavaScript, and more with our expert coding instructors. Students build real projects, develop logical thinking, and prepare for the digital economy.

  • Python & JavaScript fundamentals
  • Web development basics
  • Problem-solving & algorithms
  • Project-based learning
💃

Dance & Performing Arts

Explore the art of dance with our expert instructors. From classical Bharatanatyam to contemporary forms, we offer a variety of styles that celebrate India's rich cultural heritage.

  • Classical & folk dance forms
  • Annual cultural performances
  • Stage confidence building
  • Music & rhythm training
🗣️

Spoken English

Improve communication skills with our interactive spoken English classes. We focus on fluency, confidence, and effective expression — skills that open doors in today's world.

  • Interactive conversation sessions
  • Pronunciation & grammar
  • Debate & public speaking
  • Interview preparation
⚽

Sports & Athletics

Participate in exciting sports events and competitions to stay active, healthy, and competitive. Our annual Sports Day brings the entire school community together in celebration.

  • Annual Sports Day events
  • Cricket, football, kabaddi
  • Athletics & track events
  • Indoor games facility
🔬

Science Exhibition

Showcase innovative projects and experiments at our annual science exhibition. Students learn by doing — from hypothesis to presentation, every step builds scientific temperament.

  • Annual science fair
  • Hands-on lab experiments
  • STEM project mentoring
  • Inter-school competitions
🏅

Rajya Puraskar – Scouting

Honoring outstanding achievements in scouting and guiding with the prestigious Rajya Puraskar. This program builds leadership, discipline, and community service values in students.

  • Scout & Guide training
  • Community service projects
  • Leadership development
  • Rajya Puraskar award track
👨‍👩‍👧

Parent Competitions

We believe parents are partners in education. Our fun parent competition events strengthen bonds between family and school — fostering a true community spirit.

  • Annual parent-child events
  • Cultural participation
  • Family sports contests
  • Community celebrations
🎨

Art & Creativity

Nurturing creative expression through drawing, painting, craft, and design. Art education develops aesthetic sense, fine motor skills, and unique ways of seeing the world.

  • Drawing & painting classes
  • Craft & DIY projects
  • Art exhibitions
  • Creative competitions
