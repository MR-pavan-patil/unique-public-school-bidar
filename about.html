About Our School

Meet the dedicated people behind Unique Public School, Bidar

Principal
Leadership

Mrs. Rajeshree M. Bhalke

"Every child deserves an education that unlocks their full potential."

Our principal, Mrs. Rajeshree M. Bhalke, is a dedicated and passionate educator with a vision to provide quality education to all. With 24 years of experience in teaching and administration, she has been instrumental in shaping the academic journey of our students.

Under her leadership, Unique Public School has grown into a reputable institution, known for its academic excellence and holistic approach to education. Her commitment to education is evident in the school's curriculum, which emphasises not only academic achievement but also character development and extracurricular activities.

Mrs. Bhalke is a strong advocate for student-centered learning and believes in empowering students to take ownership of their education. She is always available to support students, parents, and teachers, and her door is always open to new ideas and suggestions.

Backbone of UPS

Mr. Maruthi Bhalke

"A supportive home is the foundation of every great institution."

We owe a great debt of gratitude to Mr. Maruthi Bhalke, a retired Assistant General Manager (AGM) from BSNL. He has been the rock-solid support system for our principal, Mrs. Rajeshree M. Bhalke. Throughout his career, Mr. Bhalke demonstrated exceptional leadership and management skills.

His guidance and expertise have been invaluable in shaping Unique Public School into the reputable institution it is today. Mr. Bhalke's unwavering support and encouragement have empowered Mrs. Bhalke to fulfill her dreams and vision for the school.

His contributions have been instrumental in creating a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, character development, and extracurricular growth.
